UDDI stands for Universal Description, Discovery, and Integration. It is an **older web services** standard used to publish and discover information about businesses and their web services


| UDDI structure       | What it represents                                       | Simple meaning                                          |
| -------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| `businessEntity`     | Information about an organization                        | “Who provides the service?”                             |
| `businessService`    | A group of related services offered by that organization | “What services do they offer?”                          |
| `bindingTemplate`    | Technical access details for a service                   | “How do I connect to it?”                               |
| `tModel`             | A reusable technical model or specification              | “What standard, interface, or category does it follow?” |
| `publisherAssertion` | A relationship between two businesses                    | “How are these organizations related?”                  |
